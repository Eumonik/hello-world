Hosting & Security:

Currently, Eumonik is hosting eumonik.com through Squarespace with a very simple landing page and social media links. Additionally, the domain name eumonik.com was purchased through Namecheap, which also offers hosting. There is no present need for customer accounts, although this would be a necessary capability in the future. The largest lift for hosting is the collection of music, which is insignificant compared to other file types. Videos will be hosted on youtube.com and embedded or linked in the appropriate pages.

As mentioned earlier, the name Eumonik is a portmanteau of euphonios and mons, which is Latin for mountains. The name was a call back to a previous alias "Symphonic", before the days of securing a unique domain name and social media handle. Eumonik was invented to fill the need for a brand name and secure a rightful identification on the Internet. More poetic, one could translate "euphonious mons" as "the good-sounding mountain" or "the mountain of good sound". I am from Colorado and have long missed the sacred song of the Rocky Mountains.
